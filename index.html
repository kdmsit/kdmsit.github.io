Hola! I'm a researcher working at the intersection of Graph Representation Learning, Geometric Deep Learning, and Generative Modeling, with a strong focus on AI for Science and AI for Medicine. If pronunciation feels tricky, call me KD.

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate at Yale University, working under Prof. Smita Krishnaswamy. Before this, I was a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) at IIT Kharagpur (2020–2026), as part of the Complex Networks Research Group (CNeRG), advised by Prof. Niloy Ganguly and Prof. Pawan Goyal.

I completed my M.Tech from the Department of CSA, IISc Bangalore (advised by Prof. M. N. Murty) and my B.Tech from Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, Kolkata. Before academia I worked at ISRO, Tata Consultancy Services, and Accenture. I also create educational content on YouTube.
